Why Mazzaella?

First, what is Mazzaella? Mazzaella is a genus of a species of seaweed. See this article. Why did I choose the domain name Mazzaella? Because it reminds me of home, the Pacific Northwest. Mazzaella splendens is a beautiful red seaweed. When you view it underwater, it shimmers.

This is a picture I took of what I hope is of Mazzaella splendens. It looks similar to it, but the iridescence did not show up on camera, so I'm not one hundred percent sure.
